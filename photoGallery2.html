The client can order a postcard set, photo book, framed photograph or canvas photograph. They can add the item to the cart and the cart will automatically calculate the subtotal, tax, and total. When the client clicks the "Enter Credit Card" button it leads the client to a page where they can enter a credit card number. The credit card number is validated based on the first number, the credit card type selected, and the Luhn algorithm.
